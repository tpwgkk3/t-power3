Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-08

Welcome to TPower3. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, username, hashed password and other details you provide when registering.
  • Payment and transaction data: payment identifiers, transaction amounts and timestamps. We do not store full credit/debit card numbers on our servers.
  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, referring URL, and time stamps to help improve site performance.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: cookies, local storage and similar tools to remember preferences and measure site usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain your account and services.
  • To process deposits, withdrawals and other payment operations securely.
  • To communicate with you about account activity, updates, and support requests.
  • To detect, prevent and respond to fraud, abuse, and other security risks.
  • To analyze and improve our products, user experience and site performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including regulations applicable to online gaming in relevant jurisdictions.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide core site functionality and to understand how visitors use the site. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the user experience.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data with:

  • Service providers and payment processors who perform services on our behalf.
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of users.
  • Affiliated partners for the purpose of delivering services when you have consented to such sharing.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is completely secure — please take care with your account credentials and report any suspicious activity to support immediately.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also opt out of marketing communications. To exercise any data rights, contact us at the address below and we will respond per applicable law.

7. Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods vary by data type and legal requirements.

8. Children

Our services are intended for users who are of legal gambling age in their jurisdictions. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

9. International Transfers

TPower3 may transfer and store information on servers located in different countries. When we transfer personal data across borders we take steps to protect it in accordance with this policy and applicable laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
